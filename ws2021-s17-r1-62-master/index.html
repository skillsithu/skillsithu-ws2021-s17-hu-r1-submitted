Environments

Citizen Park is being built in the center of Angyalföld on a quiet, leafy street, in Jász utca.

The 13th District is one of the fastest developing areas in Budapest. The excellent transport links and renewed infrastructure makes Angyalföld a very desirable area for house-hunters.

The neighbourhood is family-friendly and boasts a range of community facilities, amenities and open spaces: shops, playgrounds, kindergartens, schools, a park and a pharmacy. Furthermore, there is quick and easy access to the city centre by car, through Lehel út and Róbert Károly körút, or by using the regular bus and tram services; giving the residents of Citizen Park the best of both worlds – the tranquility of Angyalföld and the hustle and bustle of the city centre.
