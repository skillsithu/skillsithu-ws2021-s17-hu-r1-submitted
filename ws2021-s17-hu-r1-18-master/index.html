Enviroments

Citizen Park is being build in the center of Angyalföld on a quiet, leafy street, in Jász utca.

The 13rd District is one of the fastest developing areas in Budapest. The excellent transport links and renewed infrastructure makes Angyalföld a very desirable area for home-hunters.

The neightbourhood is family-friendly and boasts a range of community facilites, aminenities and open spaces; shops, playgrounds, kindergartens, schools, a park and a pharmacy.
Furthermore, there is a quick and easy access to the city centre by car, through Lehel őt and Róber Károly körút, or by using the regular bus and tram service; giving besides of the Citizen Park is the best of both worlds - the tranquality of Angyalföld and the hustle and buste of the city centre.
